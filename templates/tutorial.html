Bread Tutorial

Making your first loaf

Sourdough bread isn't the easiest to make. But because it has a lower glycemic index it is healthier for you, and because it has a bit of sourness, it tastes a whole lot better than yeasted bread, especially if you make it yourself.

Next

First, you need to assemble your ingredients

Make sure you use quality flour, since this is the main ingredient in your bread.

Measure out 500g flour into a bowl, pour in 375g water, as well as 150g sourdough.

Mix these ingredients together until they come together and set aside for twenty minutes

Measure out 10 grams of sea salt and set aside.

At the end of the 20 minutes, pour the salt in and mix for about a half minute.

Although we are not going to knead our dough, we are also not going to no-knead it.
