Troubleshooting

Dough not rising

Sometimes Sourdough needs a little bit more time than usual. Give it more time.

Dough comes apart

If the dough was elastic and pliable, and is now come apart, either you overmixed it, which is easy to do by machine, or you left it to rise way too long. In which case, take 300 grams and start over.

Dough very ugly

There's no helping this one, some doughs are born naturally ugly.

Dough rising too fast

The room is too warm. Move the dough to a cooler room.
